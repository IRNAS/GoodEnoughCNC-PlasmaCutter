GoodEnoughCNC Maker Week

Interested in attending? Subscribe and we'll get back to you when we'll have a detailed programme ready.

What is the idea?

On a week-long workshop you’ll build two GoodEnoughCNC machines, Hybrid and Troublemaker 3D printer, learn how to operate them, find out everything you need to know about digital fabrication and go home with your own “machine in a suitcase”.

  1. You come to Maribor in October 2016.
  2. You learn how to build GoodnoughCNC hybrid and Troublemaker 3d printer.
  3. You learn how to operate GoodEnooughCNC machines.
  4. You learn everything you need to know about digital fabrication technology and processes.
  5. You meet makers from all around the world, share the experiences, learn from each other and have fun.
  6. You work on example projects on GoodEnoughCNC machines to learn about different use cases.
  7. You learn about applications of open hardware and digital fabrication in different fields (biology and other).
  8. You go home with one or two machines in a suitcase (Hybrid or 3D printer kit)
  9. You build your own machine when you get home and use it for your amazing projects!

For whom?

  1. Makers, who need the machine for their work – idea testing, prototyping, small series production.
  2. Maker space owners, who want to gain all the necessary knowledge and skills to build machines for their space and/or hold workshops for their communities.
  3. For educators, who want to gain knowledge about digital fabrication to use it at their teaching.
  4. Everyone else interested in acquiring new knowledge, no prevous experience required, just curiosity about digital fabrication and making.
