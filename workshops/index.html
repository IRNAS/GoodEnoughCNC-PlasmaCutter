Workshop: GoodEnoughCNC hybrid

On a day long workshop you'll build an affordable and versatile CNC machine, learn about its operation, get it up and running and try it out with different tools. We'll walk you step-by-step through the assembly process and will be available for all your questions.

Find out more

Workshop: Troublemaker 3D printer

On a day long workshop you will assemble your own working 3D printer and gain a lot of knowledge about its operation. Starting from full kit of materials you'll step by step assemble 3D printer with the help of our mentors, then calibrate it and make your first 3D print with it.

Find out more

GoodEnoughCNC Academy

Our week long GoodenoughCNC academy program will empower you to take open hardware out to the field and build fabrication capabilities anywhere around the globe. You will learn all the necessary skills to build, operate and maintain two GoodEnoughCNC machines, Hybrid and Troublemaker 3D printer. Thus you will be empowered to take these designs anywhere around the globe and replicate them there with a GoodenoughCNC Hybrid in a suitcase kit.
